Reports

A central place to organize all 19 report views we are presenting for the case study prototype.

Safety & Compliance

4 reports
R1 Safety

Vaccination Status Report

Evaluates expired or missing vaccinations and determines grooming eligibility before service.

R2 Safety

Pet Behavioral & Incident Risk Analysis

Tracks grooming-related behavioral incidents to assess risk levels, improve safety, and support scheduling for high-risk pets.

R9 Safety

Photo Compliance Report

Monitors whether groomers follow the required before-and-after photo rule for every grooming service order.

R14 Safety

Data Quality & Missing Information Report

Identifies missing or incomplete customer and pet information to improve record accuracy and reduce service-order errors.

Operations

6 reports
R3 Operations

Daily Appointment Scheduling

Provides a chronological view of the day’s workflow for front-desk staff and groomers preparing for incoming pets.

R8 Operations

Daily Capacity and Workload Management Report

Provides a daily overview of shop capacity and groomer workload to support staffing decisions and safe operating levels.

R13 Operations

System Issue & Error Tracking Report

Tracks system errors and daily operational issues to identify recurring problems and improve workflow reliability.

R18 Operations

Service Order Exception and Approval Actions Report

Identifies service orders needing warnings, exceptions, or approval actions to support policy consistency and safer decisions.

R17 Operations

Intake Estimate Accuracy and Price Adjustment Report

Compares intake estimates with final charges to track pricing accuracy, add-ons, and special fee adjustments.

Revenue & Marketing

5 reports
R4 Revenue

Service Popularity Analysis Report

Identifies which services are trending and provides the data needed for targeted marketing campaigns.

R5 Revenue

Customer Retention & Inactive Clients Report

Identifies customers who have not returned within a specific timeframe to support promotions and repeat business.

R7 Revenue

Revenue and Sales Breakdown Report

Provides a financial summary of grooming service orders to monitor revenue performance and identify high-performing services.

R12 Revenue

Add-On Service Usage Report

Shows which add-on services are selected most often, which generate the most revenue, and which are trending up or down.
R19 Revenue

Package Service Usage Report

Highlights the most popular grooming packages, package revenue, and package trends during the selected reporting period.
R16 Revenue

New and Returning Customer Monthly Report

Compares the number of new and returning customers monthly to evaluate client growth and business goals.

Staff

2 reports
R6 Staff

Groomer Performance & Productivity Report

Evaluates groomer workload, efficiency, and revenue contribution to help optimize staffing and scheduling.

R11 Staff

Groomer Skill and Service Report

Provides an overview of groomer skills, certifications, and authorized services to support assignment and training decisions.

Pets

2 reports
R10 Pets

Special Handling and Senior Dog Report

Identifies pets needing extra time, medical consideration, behavioral accommodations, or senior-specific care.

R15 Pets

Dog Size and Service Packages Correlation

Shows which service packages are most frequently booked by dog size category to support promotions and planning.
